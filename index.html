Services

Janitorial Services for Commercial Facilities in Austin, TX

Janitorial services are our core capability. We design recurring cleaning programs around how your facility actually operates — traffic flow, compliance sensitivity, security protocols, and the appearance standards your tenants and visitors expect.

Core Janitorial Capabilities

Office Cleaning

Recurring nightly, daytime, and weekend programs scoped to your tenant mix, footprint, and appearance standards — from single-suite offices to multi-floor corporate campuses.

  • Workstation, conference room, and breakroom detail
  • Restroom sanitation with par-level consumables
  • Hard floor and entryway care, glass and high-touch disinfection
  • Locked-building protocols, alarm sign-on/off, security badging
Day Porter Services

On-site daytime presence for Class A lobbies, common areas, and tenant-facing spaces — keeping high-traffic environments tour-ready throughout business hours.

  • Restroom rounds, glass touch-ups, and spill response
  • Trash, recycling, and breakroom resets between meetings
  • Lobby presentation, signage, and elevator cab care
  • Event setup support and tenant request fulfillment
Warehouse Cleaning

Industrial-grade cleaning programs for distribution centers, manufacturing floors, and flex-industrial buildings across the Austin metro and Central Texas corridor.

  • Ride-on and walk-behind floor scrubbing for sealed concrete
  • Dock door, loading bay, and staging area maintenance
  • High-bay dust control, rack wiping, and overhead cleaning
  • Office trailer, breakroom, and restroom programs
Apartment Trash Valet

Doorstep trash and recycling collection for multifamily, student, and mixed-use communities — a five-night-a-week amenity that reduces resident complaints and chute traffic.

  • Five- to seven-night pickup windows, weather-resilient
  • Uniformed valets, branded carts, and route accountability
  • Recycling separation and bulk-item escalation
  • Photo-logged compliance for non-conforming sets
Supply Management

Turnkey consumables management so your facility team never runs out of paper, soap, or liners — and never overpays for them.

  • Par-level inventory with on-site closet audits
  • Volume-based sourcing for towels, tissue, and can liners
  • Dispenser standardization and refill scheduling
  • Consolidated monthly billing with usage reporting
Supplier Support

Vendor coordination for hygiene products, dispensers, and specialty supplies — a single point of accountability across your hardware, chemicals, and equipment stack.

  • Dispenser installs, swap-outs, and warranty support
  • Specialty chemical programs (green-seal, food-safe, healthcare)
  • Equipment service coordination for scrubbers and burnishers
  • Single invoice across multiple supplier relationships

Auxiliary Services

Specialty & Project Services

Beyond recurring janitorial, we provide project-based services that complement a clean program —

Scheduled strip-and-wax, scrub-and-recoat, and burnishing programs for VCT, vinyl, and resilient hard surface flooring. We work nights and weekends to minimize disruption and protect adjacent finishes.

Hot-water extraction, encapsulation cleaning, and spot treatment for corporate offices, hospitality, and high-traffic commercial carpet. Scheduled programs and as-needed project cleans available.

Touch-up, repaints, and refresh projects for office interiors, common areas, and tenant turnovers. Coordinated with property management schedules and tenant access windows.

Exterior pressure washing for sidewalks, building facades, parking garages, dumpster pads, drive-thrus, and entryway surfaces. We use appropriate PSI and surface-cleaner attachments to clean efficiently without damaging substrates, and we coordinate after-hours scheduling to keep tenants and customers unaffected.

Decorative and protective concrete staining for warehouses, showrooms, retail interiors, patios, and commercial entries. Surface preparation, acid or water-based stain application, and sealing produce a durable, easy-to-maintain finish that holds up to heavy commercial traffic.

Commercial flooring installation across LVP, VCT, sheet vinyl, and carpet tile for offices, multifamily turnovers, and tenant improvements. Subfloor prep, transitions, and base coordination are handled in-scope so the finished space is move-in ready.

Texan Sun is built to be a long-term cleaning partner for the Austin construction community. We support general contractors, builders, commercial construction firms, tenant improvement contractors, and project managers with dependable post-construction cleaning that hits turnover deadlines.

  • Rough, final, and detail post-construction cleans
  • Touch-up cleaning during punch-list and walkthroughs
  • Turnover cleaning before owner acceptance or tenant move-in
  • Coordination with site supers and project schedules
  • Repeatable, documented scope for multi-phase projects

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas of Austin does Texan Sun serve?

We are based in Southeast Austin near the airport business park corridor and serve the full Austin metro — including the Central Business District, The Domain, North and Northwest Austin, Round Rock, Cedar Park, Georgetown, Pflugerville, San Marcos, Kyle, and Buda. Multi-site coverage is available in Dallas-Fort Worth, Houston, and San Antonio.

What types of facilities do you clean?

Our commercial janitorial programs cover corporate offices, healthcare facilities, manufacturing plants, warehouses and distribution centers, hospitality properties, schools, and multi-tenant commercial buildings. We can also support multifamily common areas and amenity spaces.

Month-to-month or yearly contract?

Our standard agreement is month-to-month with an annual review to ensure continued quality and satisfaction. However, if your facility requires a yearly contract with renewal options, we’re happy to accommodate that as well.

Is janitorial pricing based on square footage or scope of work?

For recurring services, our standard pricing is based on the scope of work. However, we can also accommodate pricing based on square footage if preferred.

Do you provide day porter services?

Yes. Day porter services are available for high-traffic lobbies, common areas, restrooms, and amenity spaces — typically in Class A office buildings, hospitality, and multi-tenant properties where appearance must be maintained throughout the business day.

Can you manage janitorial supplies for our building?

We offer consumables management, par-level monitoring, and supplier coordination as part of our janitorial programs. That includes paper products, soaps, dispensers, and specialty hygiene supplies appropriate to your facility type.
